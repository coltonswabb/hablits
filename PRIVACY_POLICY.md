# Privacy Policy for Hablits

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Overview

Hablits ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection and Storage

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

All data created in Hablits, including but not limited to:
- Habit tracking data
- Journal entries and notes
- Completion logs and statistics
- Settings and preferences
- Pet customizations
- Identity filters
- Fasting timer data

...is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## Data You Control

### Local Storage
- All app data is stored locally on your device
- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete all data at any time through the app settings
- Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

### Data Export
- You can export your data as a JSON file at any time
- Exported data is saved to your device's file system
- You control where exported files are saved and shared

## Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications:
- Daily habit reminders at times you configure
- Fasting timer completion alerts

These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

## Third-Party Services

Hablits does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Hablits does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed to be used by individuals of all ages and stores all data locally on the device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Data Security

While we do not collect your data, we recommend:
- Keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock
- Regularly backing up your device
- Using the app's export feature to backup your habit data

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have full access to your data at all times
- You can export your data at any time
- You can delete your data at any time
- You have complete control over your information

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub: https://github.com/anthropics/hablits/issues

## Legal Basis

This app operates under the principle of data minimization - we simply do not collect or process any personal data beyond what is stored locally on your device under your control.
