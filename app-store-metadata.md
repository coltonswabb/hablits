## Full Description

**Build Better Habits with Hablits**

Hablits is a beautiful, minimalist habit tracker that makes building positive routines fun and rewarding. Track your daily habits, customize your virtual pet companion, and watch your progress grow over time.

**Key Features:**

• **Simple Habit Tracking** - Log daily habits with a single tap
• **Virtual Pet Companion** - Choose from 12 unique pet species that react to your progress
• **Beautiful Themes** - 13 stunning themes including light, dark, retro, gameboy, cosmic, and more
• **Pet Customization** - Dress up your pet with 20+ fun hats and accessories
• **Weekly & Calendar Views** - Visualize your consistency at a glance
• **Habit Categories** - Organize habits by identity (Health, Productivity, etc.)
• **Day Planner** - Schedule your habits and routines
• **Stats & Insights** - Track streaks, completion rates, and progress
• **Notes & Journaling** - Add notes to any day to track context
• **100% Private** - All data stays on your device, no account required
• **Offline First** - Works perfectly without internet connection

**Perfect For:**

- Building morning/evening routines
- Tracking fitness and health goals
- Developing creative practices
- Managing productivity habits
- Creating positive lifestyle changes

**Why Hablits?**

Unlike other habit trackers, Hablits focuses on simplicity and delight. Your customizable pet companion provides gentle motivation without guilt or pressure. With beautiful themes and smooth animations, checking in on your habits becomes something you look forward to.

**Privacy First**

Your data never leaves your device. No account required, no tracking, no ads. Just you and your habits.

Download Hablits today and start building the life you want, one habit at a time.
