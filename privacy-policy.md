# Privacy Policy for Hablits

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction

Hablits ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Hablits mobile application.

## Data Collection and Storage

**Local Storage Only**: Hablits stores all of your data locally on your device using React Native's AsyncStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device:
- Your habit tracking data (habits, completion logs, notes)
- Your identity/category settings
- Your theme and pet customization preferences
- Your app settings (sound effects, etc.)

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not sell or monetize your data

## Third-Party Services

Hablits does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising.

## Data Control

- **Your Data, Your Control**: All your data remains on your device
- **Data Deletion**: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data
- **No Account Required**: Hablits does not require account creation or login

## Children's Privacy

Hablits does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device, we have no access to user data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@hablits.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/hablits/hablits

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any of your data on our servers, you have complete control over your information. You can:
- Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
- Export your data using the app's export feature
- Control what data you enter into the app

By using Hablits, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
